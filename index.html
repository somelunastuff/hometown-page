Paris of the north, discover the Arctic Jewel

Top three activities to do in Tromsø

Hop on the mountain lift!

From the top of the mountain you have a perfect view of the whole city, the northern lights in the winter, and the midnight sun in the summer.

Take a trip to sommarøy!

Remember to bring your swimwear for a dip in the chilly water, or your hiking clothes to enjoy the breathtaking views of the beaches from up high.

Climb the Sherpa stairs!

The Sherpa Stairs offer a workout with a rewaring payoff: stunning city and sky views. Grab a delicious waffle at the top café before heading down.
